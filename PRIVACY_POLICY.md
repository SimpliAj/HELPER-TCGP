# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## 1. Introduction

HELPER-TCGP ("the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Discord Data

The Bot collects the following Discord data to function properly:

- **Guild IDs** - To identify and manage server-specific configurations
- **Channel IDs** - To send and update statistics messages in designated channels
- **User IDs** - To track command usage and manage user permissions
- **Message IDs** - To update existing messages with statistics or trading information
- **Member Information** - Guild owner/administrator data for permission validation

### 2.2 Configuration Data

We store the following information for each Discord server:

- Pack preferences and series configurations
- Channel settings for statistics messages
- Trading statistics and card tracking data
- Customized bot settings specific to each guild

### 2.3 Statistical Data

The Bot tracks:

- Message counts and engagement metrics
- Pack opening frequency statistics
- Card trading records (when manually entered)
- Guild activity patterns

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery** - To provide core Bot functionality and features
- **Improvement** - To analyze usage patterns and improve Bot features
- **Maintenance** - To debug errors and ensure service quality
- **Security** - To prevent abuse and enforce Terms of Service
- **Statistics** - To generate and display usage analytics within your guild

## 4. Data Storage

- All guild configuration and statistics data is stored locally on the Bot's host server
- Data is stored in JSON format within the Bot's operational directory
- Each guild's data is stored separately in guild-specific configuration files
- Data persists until manually deleted or the Bot is deactivated

## 5. Data Retention

- Configuration data is retained as long as the Bot is active in a guild
- When the Bot leaves a guild, data may be retained for a period to allow re-activation
- Users may request complete data deletion by having a server administrator use the configuration reset command
- Statistics data is cleared when a guild's configuration is reset

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

- Access to configuration files is restricted to the Bot process
- Data is not transmitted to external services without explicit user consent
- The Bot does not store sensitive information such as passwords or payment details

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 7. Third-Party Services

The Bot may interact with:

- **Discord API** - For core bot functionality (subject to Discord's privacy policy)
- **Error Logging Webhooks** - Optional error reporting to help improve the Bot
- **No External Analytics** - We do not use third-party analytics services

## 8. User Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** - Request access to all data we hold about your server
- **Correction** - Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion** - Request deletion of your guild's configuration through the configuration reset command
- **Portability** - Export your statistics data in JSON format

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to the Bot, please contact us immediately.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through Discord announcements or GitHub notifications. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 11. Contact & Data Rights

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights:

- Contact the Bot developer through Discord
- Open an issue on the GitHub repository
- Request access to or deletion of your guild's data through Bot commands

## 12. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union:

- You have the right to request a copy of your personal data
- You have the right to request erasure of your personal data
- You have the right to restrict processing of your personal data
- You may withdraw consent at any time
- You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

Requests for data access or deletion can be made to the Bot developer.

## 13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident:

- You have the right to know what personal information is collected
- You have the right to know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
- You have the right to delete personal information collected from you
- You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information

The Bot does not sell personal information.
